We've learned from our experiences as students to put together the best products to get you ready to move into college. Did we mention it's all on amazon too?

Kitchen

Everything you need to bring the kitchen into the dorm.

Mini Fridge

Make sure to check with your roommate first, but no judgement for those who just need their own fridge.

Microwave

Again check with your roommate and what your college allows, but the microwave can be the essential cooking tool for college

Electric Kettle

Live on tea or coffee? or going to the school in the north? This is a must have.

Coffee Maker (Aeropress)

Buying Starbucks everyday can get expensive, and the aeropress is a coffee game changer. Fast, delicious coffee and even faster clean up, all in the comfort of your dorm. No waking up early just to get coffee before class.

Keurig

Now this is an item for those who live on their hot drinks. Literally no clean up and you'll be serving up the best drinks. Another item that could make you many friends on your hall.

Blender

These can be loud if you're using them in the morning, but sometime a smoothie or protein shake is just what hits the spot.

Utensils

A simple knife, fork, and spoon for when you need to grab a meal in your room.

Plates

simple plates and a bowl for when you just want to stay in your room to eat or heat up leftovers in the microwave.

Travel Mug

This is going to keep your hot or cold drinks at the perfect temperature all day long through all your classes.

Water Bottle

This is the best designed water bottle we've seen, and it has a filter so you can fill it up anywhere on campus you happen to be.

Bedding

You spend 1/3 of your time in your bed (maybe more maybe less in college), it's worth getting the good stuff.

Mattress Pad

There's no point in trying to hide this. The mattresses in college are usually just a little thicker than cardboard, making getting a good mattress pad essential. Trust us, you'll thank us for this one.

Comforter

This comforter is amazing, it won't get you sweaty on hotter night but it sure will keep you toasty warm through the winter.

Duvet Cover Set

A great set in a lot of colors, but feel free to go out and find one that fits your personality! The comforter and cover are twin size (don't worry about getting twin xl for the comforter, it'll fit just fine)

Sheets

You'll probably want to grab at least 2 of these. you'll probably want to switch your sheets more often than you'll want to do laundry and always good to change up the color.

Ear Plugs

We hope you don't regularly need these, but chances are there will be a few times you need to block out noise and you'll be glad these were sitting in your drawer.

Room

All the essentials to keep your dorm looking good, functional, and clean.

Vacuum

Some dorms have vacuums you can rent but from experience its usually a hassle and half broken. both you and all your hall-mates will be happy you have this.

Hangers

Plain and simple, grab a bunch of these to hang your clothes (and maybe grab some more if you hate folding)

Hooks

Again no holes in the wall, so these are great to make hooks for towels, bags, coats, the possibilities are endless.

Poster Strips

You won't be allowed to put any holes in the wall, so grab these to put up all your posters and pictures. White walls can get depressing.

First Aid Kit

An item we really hope you won't need but you'll be really glad when you do. Definitely worth getting.

Dry Erase Cork Board

Stay organized with all your work and sheets with putting it all on the wall so you can see it everyday. To many of us put important stuff in our desk and never remember it.

Extra Chair

No better way to make friends than to have extra seats in your dorm.

Bathroom

Everything you need to keep up your hygiene in college

Towels

A great set in lots of different colors, with 2 bath towels, 2 hand towels, and 2 wash clothes. All you need in one pack.

Shower Caddy

Nothing special here, just a good looking shower tote that will get the job done of transporting all your toiletries to the bathroom.

Shower Flip Flops

These are just a must for communal bathrooms. And please don't be the kid who shows up with non rubber flip flops that just soak up all the soapy water and leaves a trail back to your dorm. (We also like the flip flops at old navy for a couple bucks)

Laundry

Electronics
